About

Shumei Wiggins, CMP

A former nurse, Shumei Wiggins is a licensed Manicurist/Pedicurist with more than a decade of professional experience. Ever since she started her nail tech career, Shumei has been maintaining up-to-date knowledge of manicure and pedicure trends and styles by constantly seeking advanced training. Shumei always wants to create more meaningful work combining the practical aspects of nail care with the playful side of creativity in nail art. She is currently working on becoming a Certified MediNail-Medical Nail Technician™ (MNT).

Safety Measures

  • Single-use items (paper files, buffers, nail drill sanding bands, cotton balls, paraffin wax) are discarded after single use.
  • Metal implements and towels are cleaned and steam autoclave sterilized after each use.
  • The station is thoroughly disinfected before and after each client.
  • We wear gloves and perform hand hygiene before donning and after removing.
  • Masks are always required to be worn by employees and customers in the salon.
  • We take your temperature using a touchless infra-red thermometer.
  • We ask some screening questions relating to COVID-19 symptoms.
  • Social distancing in effect - maintain 6 feet of distance at all times.
  • Please use hand sanitizer upon entering the salon, restroom, and rooms.
  • Please reschedule if anyone in your household has been sick in the last 2 weeks.

Policies

Appointments

Appointments are highly recommended to better ensure staff availability. To schedule an appointment, text us at 281.739.9138. The more time we are given in advance, the more likely we will be able to accommodate your date request.

Walk-ins

Walk-ins and same-day appointments may be available but are not guaranteed. To schedule an appointment and to check our availability, text us at 281.739.9138.

Confirmations

As a courtesy, we will send a text to confirm your service appointments one day before your appointment date. If we have not received a text response, we will attempt a phone call. If we are unable to reach you, and can only leave a message, please understand that it is your responsibility to remember your appointment dates and times to avoid late arrivals, missed appointments, and the cancellation fee.

Cancellations, Re-schedule, No-show

Your appointments are very important to us. Each appointment is reserved especially for you. We do understand that sometimes schedule adjustments are necessary; therefore, we respectfully request 24 hours’ notice for adjustments to your appointments and for cancellations.

Please understand that when you forget or cancel your appointment without giving enough notice, we miss the opportunity to fill that appointment time, and clients on our waiting list miss the opportunity to receive services. Our appointments are confirmed one day in advance because we know it is possible to forget an appointment you booked months ago. Since the appointment is reserved especially for you, same day cancellations, re-schedule or no-show will be charged full price of the scheduled service.

If you miss/cancel (same day cancellation) two appointments, your client file will be red flagged which means we will need a credit card to secure your next appointment. We reserve the right to require prepayment.

Late Arrivals

As a courtesy to all clients scheduled throughout the day, on-time arrival is expected.

To remain on schedule for all our clients, we may shorten your service time if you arrive more than 15 minutes late for your appointment. The service price will be charged in full.

In the case that due to your late arrival, we need to reschedule your appointment in consideration of the next client, you will still be responsible for the full-service fee of your originally scheduled appointment.

Any “late” arrival will be documented under your client file. Repetitive behaviors may result in prepayment requirement for future appointments.

Refusal of Service

We have the right to refuse service to anyone at any time with no refund. This includes but is not limited to the usage of drugs, alcohol, sexual comments/remarks, or any unacceptable and disruptive behavior that we feel is inappropriate. As licensed professionals, we follow a strict code of ethics. It is important that a safe environment is maintained for us and each of our clients.
