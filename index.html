Shumei Wiggins, CMP


A former nurse, Shumei Wiggins is a licensed Manicurist/Pedicurist with more than a decade of professional experience. Ever since she started her nail tech career, Shumei has been advancing her knowledge and skills by constantly seeking advanced hands-on training. She always wants to create more meaningful work combining the practical aspects of nail care with the playful side of creativity in nail art.

For Shumei, the most fulfilling part of being a nail technician is seeing the smiles on her clients' faces when their nail-related issues are been solved. She is currently working on becoming a Certified MediNail-Medical Nail Technician™ (MNT).
